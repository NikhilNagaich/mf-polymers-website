About SmartForge

Revolutionizing precision plastic manufacturing through innovative SmartForge technology that bridges the gap between R&D and production.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to democratize access to precision plastic manufacturing. Traditional micro-injection molding requires months of iterative development, multiple mold modifications, and significant upfront costs.

Our SmartForge technology eliminates these barriers, enabling companies to go from prototype to production-ready parts in a fraction of the time and cost.
